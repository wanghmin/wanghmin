RESEARCH SUMMARY



The fast development of virtual reality and digital manufacturing industries is quickly redefining what computer graphics is and what computer graphics research should be. In this rapid transition, it is imperative to have fast and reliable computational tools for real-time simulation and modeling of solids, shells, and fluids. Toward this goal, I focus my research in four major directions. Below are the papers highlighting my research in each direction. Please click the links to read more.
